Arcane Feats

Arcane Feats comes from your practicing and learning of magic. You can pick one new arcane feat each level and retrain them during downtime.

Feat Name Description Prerequisites Level
Abjure Accident You've trained to abjure a spellshaping failure, one of the deadliest threats to mages. You learn the Abjure Accident Reaction.
Abjure Accident. 1 Reaction when you get a failure, but not a critical failure on a spellshaping check.
Prevent the effect of the spellshaping failure. 		None 1
Adept Elemental Magic Can be chosen multiple times, once for each aspect. Choose one aspect and learn two adept rank spells from that aspect. You gain a bonus depending on the aspect
  • Air: After you cast an air spell you ignore difficult terrain for the turn
  • Earth: After you cast an earth spell you have a +3 bonus to saving throws against being knocked prone, off-guard or moved against your will for the rest of the turn
  • Fire: You learn the fuel spell metamagic, fueling spells with your inner heat.
  • Water: +1 to saving throws against being poisoned or diseased
  • Light: +1 to saving throws against being blinded or dazzled
  • Darkness: After you cast an darkness spell, you gain darkvision for the turn
  • Metal: After you cast an metal spell you gain resistance 3 to physical damage for the rest of turn
  • Ice: You ignore difficult terrain from ice and snow
 Basic Elemental Magic, for the same aspect 4
Alchemy Training You are trained in alchemy. Each morning you can make two temporary potions. For each potion you prepare you lock away 2 mana from your mana well for the day and they expire after the day. The potions can be lesser health potions, lesser mana potions or lesser elemental resistance potions. None 1
Aura Reinforcement You have trained your aura and can raise and lower it as a single action. When you use the Reinforce Aura action you can spend mana up to 3 times your level + your Will. None 1
Basic Elemental Magic Can be chosen multiple times, once for each aspect. Choose one aspect and learn two apprentice rank spells from that aspect. You have +1 to spellcasting when casting spells of your chosen aspect. None 1
Focused Spellshaping You have a +1 to spellshaping when casting spells that cost 3 actions or more. Learn two apprentices spells that costs 3 actions or more. None 1
Freeform Learning You learn four apprentice spells of your choosing. None 1
Gadget Crafting You are trained in crafting. Each morning you can make one temporary gadget. If you do you lock away 4 mana from your mana well for the day and the gadget only works for you. None 1
Monster Hunting You are trained in monster hunting lore, which can be used to recall knowledge against monsters and elementals. When you succeed on a recall knowledge with monster hunting lore, the creature takes a -2 penalty to the next save you impose on it. None 1
Metamagic Adept You learn three metamagics of your choice.
You may use multiple metamagics on a single spell		 Metamagic Novice Feat 4
Metamagic Novice You learn two metamagics of your choice. None 1
Staff Training You are trained with channeling staves and battlestaves. You can provide somatic components with hand even if your holding a staff in it. None 1

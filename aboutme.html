About Me

I grew up in the city of Norrköping, moved to Stockholm when I was 13 and studied game design in Skövde for 6 years, getting a Bachelor in Game Design and a Master in Digital Narration and Cultural Heritage. Sadly that didn't give me a job. Now I'm back in Stockholm looking for work, but I am open to moving to some other part of Sweden (or even nearby EU countries) where I find a job.

When I'm not working on games or looking for a job I like to read fantasy and sci-fi books, play board games and video games, hike in nature and do some worldbuilding.

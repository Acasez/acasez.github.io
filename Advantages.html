Advantages

Advatages are powerful feats that shape your character. You pick one advantage at level 1, 4, 8, 12 and 16.

Feat Name Description Prerequisites Level
Attunement You have an attunement, a magical connection through your soul to one aspect of magic. This makes it easier to cast that type of magic, but prevents you from casting the opposite aspect. Spells from your attuned aspect are called attuned spells
  • While casting attuned spells, reduce their mana cost by your level
  • While casting attuned spells, you may also use your WIL instead of INT for the spellshaping check.
  • You learn one apprentice spell of your attunement.
  • You cannot learn or cast spells of the opposite aspect from your attunement
 1
Crafting Prodigy You are specialized in one field of crafting above everything else and are able to craft things that would normally be above your level of knowlegde. You learn one of Golemcrafter, Armor Artificer, or Runegun Smith feats, ignoring their level requirements. When picking new feats you treat feats from your chosen archetype as if their level requirements were 2 levels lower.

Additionally you become trained in crafting and have a +3 bonus to any checks regarding your field. 		1
Swift You have 1 tile extra movement speed and may add your DEX to initiative checks. Your become trained in Reflex saves, and if another effect would make you trained you become expert instead. 1
Experienced You've spent your time doing a variety of things. Gain one more background and one more General Feat 1
Duck! Your experience tells you diving out of the way is sometime the best out of the way of powerful attacks. You have the Duck! reaction Duck! 1 Reaction, when you are forced to make a Reflex Saving Throw.
You attempt to dive out of the effect. You move 1 tile. If this causes you to leave the area of the spell you have +5 bonus to the saving throw, otherwise its a +3 bonus. After making the save you fall prone. 		1
Large Mana Well You have an larger than average mana well, allowing you to cast more spells and increasing the strength of your aura. Increase your maximum Mana by 5. Every time you level up your maximum mana increases by an additional 3. 1
Arcane Training You have spend your time studying magic to compete with those that have magical gifts. Learn two more Arcane Feats. Additionally you become trained in arcana. 1
Soulsight You posses the rare gift of natural soulsight, allowing you to see souls. Souls appear as tiny dots of light in the center of living creatures. You have the Inspect Soul action. Inspect Soul 1 Action. Target one creature you can see within 4 tiles. Looking at a creatures soul you perceive a few details about it. You learn if it has
  • An attunement, and if so which aspect.
  • A soulbond. You can make a DC 20 arcana check to try to figure out the nature of the soulbond.
  • Weather any other soul magic is affecting the target, though you don't know what type it is.
  • If the creature is an illusion, or a object or golem made to look like a creature you get no information about it, but see that it's not a creature

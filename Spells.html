Spells

List of all spells and spell traits

Spells are cast by performing a Spellshaping Check, against a DC that is based on the spells rank and tratis. The spellcaster rolls a D20 and adds their INT and DEX, and if they get above or euqal to Spellshaping DC they succed at casting the spell. Craetures rolling against the spell roll against the result of the spellshaping check.

Generally on a failed spellcasting nothing happens, but some spells have effects that take place.

Spell traits

Spells have traits that describe what they do and interact with game systems.

  • Precision When targeting weak points the penalty is 2 lower than normal (to a minimum of 1)
  • Targetting You can target weak points with this spell
  • Area The spell affects an area
  • Manipulation The spell manipulates or shapes matter
  • Complex The spellshaping DC increase by 2
  • Draining The mana cost is increased to next rank
  • Enchanment The spell temporarily inproves the caster or gives them new abilities
  • Sensing The spell helps detect things

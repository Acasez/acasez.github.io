General Feats

General feats comes from your combat and general life experience. You can pick one new combat feat every other level.

Feat Name Description Prerequisites Level
Aura Hardening Your aura is tougher and you are trained in using it for defense. When succeed with an Aura Block, the damage it takes is reduced by your WIL. You become trained in WIL saves 1
Battlefield Medic You are trained in medicine or athletics and you don't take the -2 penalty to the treat wounds and stabilize creature actions in combat 1
Combat Training Your have seen combat or trained in a army. You are trained with light armor You also become trained in Fortitude saving throws. 1
Dedicated Student You have put practicing magic as your focus ahead of all else. Gain one level 1 Arcane Feats 1
Duck! Your experience tells you diving out of the way is sometime the best out of the way of powerful attacks. You have the Duck! reaction Duck! 1 Reaction, when you are forced to make a Reflex Saving Throw.
You attempt to dive out of the effect. You move 1 tile. If this causes you to leave the area of the spell you have +5 bonus to the saving throw, otherwise its a +3 bonus. After making the save you fall prone. 		1
Duelist You have trained a bit in duels, of the arcane or mundane variety. You become trained in Reflex saving throws. When you roll initiative and aren't surprised you may add your INT or WIL to your roll. 1
Heavy Armor Training Unlike most mages you have experience in fighting in heavy armor. You are trained with heavy armor Trained in light armor 1
Inspiring Leader You have a way with words and can inspire those around you. You can use the Inspire Courage action Inspire Courage 2 Actions
You give a rousing speech or perform an act that inspires your allies. All allies in the aura gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and saving throws against fear effects for the duration. This bonus increases to +2 at level 10 and +3 at level 15. The aura lasts for one minute or until you are incapacitated. 		1
Sneaky You can move silently and hide in shadows. When sneaking you can move at full speed without taking the -5 penalty. Trained in stealth 1
Survivor You have survived against the elements. You gain resistance 3 to one type of elemental damage: fire, cold, electricity, acid or sonic. You have a +3 bonus on survival checks to avoid natural hazards and extreme weather. 1
Surveyor You have studied maps and the land, learning how to read the terrain. You become trained in geography lore. Your exploration speed is two steps faster than what it would normally be. You can use survival to navigate even in unfamiliar terrain, and you can always find north. 1
Staff Training You are trained with channeling staves and battlestaves. You can turn wood into a battlestaff during a short rest. If you have the Mold Earth or Shape Metal spells you can use it to create a battlestaff during a short rest. 1
Non Magical Fighting You have trained in non magical fighting. You become trained with simple weapons, unarmed attacks and one weapon group of your choice. 1
Take Aim You've trained in the art of aiming and shooting. You can use the Aim action Aim 1 Action
You carefully aim your next ranged attack, gaining a +2 bonus to the attack roll. The bonus lasts until you move or the start of your next turn. 		1
Gadget Crafting You are trained in crafting. Each morning you can make one temporary gadget. If you do you lock away 4 mana from your mana well for the day and the gadget only works for you. 1
Martial Arts Training You know how to fight unarmed, from time spent scrapping or formal martial arts training. You become trained with unarmed attacks. Your unarmed attacks use a one step larger dice (1 -> 1d4 -> 1d6 -> 1d8). You also become trained in Brawling Lore. 1
Skilled You have trained in two skills of your choice, becoming trained them. 1

Weapons

List of all weapons and weapon traits

Weapon attacks made with PHY rolls and are avoided by Reflex Saves, hitting if the attack roll is higher than the save. Ranged weapon attacks are made with DEX and weapons with the finesse trait can be used with either. Damage rolls always add PHY.

Mages rarely use weapons as they need their hands free for somantic components. Somantic Components, while your hands are free you can add your DEX to spellshaping checks. If you have one hand free you can add half your DEX.

Weapon traits

  • Agile. The multiple attack penalty you take with this weapon on the second attack on your turn is –4 instead of –5, and –8 instead of –10 on the third and subsequent attacks in the turn.
  • Finesse. You can use your DEX instead of PHY when making attacks with this weapon. PHY is still added to the damage roll (even if its negative)
  • Thrown (X Tiles). You can throw this weapon as a ranged attack.
  • Versatile You choose the damage type among those specified. If the damage type has a dice next to it changes the damage roll to that.
  • Shove Creatures hit by this weapon need to make a Fortitude saving throw or be moved up to 1 tile of attackers choice..
  • Channeling You can still perform somantic component with a hand holding a channeling weapon.
  • Aiming Focus When casting spells with a range over 6 tiles, their range increases by 2.
  • Precision When targeting weak points the penalty is 2 lower than normal (to a minimum of 1)
  • Parry While holding this weapon you have the Parry action.
    Parry 1 Action. Get ready to parry. When you are attacked by a melee attack until the start of next turn you may defend with your weapon attack bonus instead of your Reflex save.

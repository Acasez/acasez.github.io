Armor

List of all armors in the game.

Armor is split into light and heavy armor and provides damage resistance, but reduces your mana recovery and some dexterity based stats. Armor comes with a weak point which can be targeted, halving the armor resistance at the cost of making the attack easier to dodge.

Some armor require characters to have a certain Physique score to wear it. You take a 1 tile movement penalty for each step below the requirement.

If you don't have proficiency in the armor your wearing it's penalties is doubled and you can't add your DEX to spellshaping checks.

Hi! I'm Edvin Skogsholm Sanne, a game desinger.

My name is Edvin Skogsholm Sanne and I'm a game designer from Sweden. I've studied game design and digital narration at Högskolan i Skövde/Skövde University.
My main skills lie in gameplay, balance and level design. I have also worked on level design, programming and UI design. I have experience with scripting in Unity, with Unreal Blueprints as well as other programming languages
My favorite game genre to both design and play is strategy games, though I can work with most genres. I also really like worldbuilding, especially in fantasy and historical settings.

https://acasez.itch.io/ for all the games I've worked on.

edvin.sanne@gmail.com if you want to contact me. If there is a job you think I would fit in, don't hesitate to contact me.

Amsvartne (2024-2025)

Puzzle Platformer
Amsvartne is a 2D puzzle platformer I made as part of the start up group Corvian Games (now Tiny Creature Games).
It focuses on narrative exploration and puzzle solving using light to navigate levels and solve puzzles, set in a mystical mine in a fantasy dieselpunk setting.
I worked on the game and level design, as well as programming multiple game systems.

Group Project, Made in Unity

Mariestad Klimatspelet

Eurogame style board game about city planning and sustainability
A strategic board game about about making a mid sized Swedish city climate neutral.
In the game players play cards to build green power, nature areas to absorb pollution and other projects to achieve sustainability, while managing their income and the opinion of people in the municipality.
I proposed the original idea for the game and have worked on game design, balance and testing. I worked with with Iris Casado, Zoey Wu and Alexander Ros who worked on graphic design and card illustrations.

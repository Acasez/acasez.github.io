Hi! I'm Edvin Skogsholm Sanne, a game desinger.

My name is Edvin Skogsholm Sanne and I'm a game designer from Sweden. I've studied game design and digital narration at Högskolan i Skövde/Skövde University.
My main skills lie in gameplay, balance and level design. I have also worked on level design, programming and UI design. I have experience with scripting in Unity, with Unreal Blueprints as well as other programming languages
My favorite game genre to both design and play is strategy games, though I can work with most genres. I also really like worldbuilding, especially in fantasy and historical settings.

https://acasez.itch.io/ for all the games I've worked on.

edvin.sanne@gmail.com if you want to contact me. If there is a job you think I would fit in, don't hesitate to contact me.

Main Projects

Some of the projects I've done

AMSVARTNE (2024-2025)

Atmospheric Puzzle Platformer

Amsvartne is a 2D puzzle platformer I made as part of the start up group Corvian Games (now Tiny Creature Games). It focuses on narrative exploration and puzzle solving using light to navigate levels and solve puzzles. It's set in a mystical mine in a fantasy dieselpunk setting. I worked on the game and level design, as well as programming multiple game systems.

Group Project, Made in Unity

MARIESTAD CLIMATE BOARD GAME (2022-2024)

Eurogame style board game about city planning and sustainability

A strategic board game about making a mid sized Swedish city climate neutral. In the game players play cards to build green power, nature areas to absorb pollution and other projects to achieve sustainability, while managing their income and the opinion of people in the municipality.

I proposed the original idea for the game and have worked on game design, balance and testing. I worked with Iris Casado, Zoey Wu and Alexander Ros who worked on graphic design and card illustrations.

Group Project

Mariestad Digital Adaption (2024-2025)

Digital Adaptation of the climate board game

I also made digital, solo only, adaption of the Mariestad Board Game. Its up on itch and can be be found here:
https://acasez.itch.io/mariestad-climate-game

It has most of the features of physical board game and a inbuilt tutorial. It also the option to be played in either English and Swedish and has options for different amount of starting pollution, to provide different levels of challenge. I made a script that imports the card data using CSV files for quick playtesting and balancing, a method I use in my projects today.

Solo Project, Made in Unity

ION Games Internship (2024)

Internship at ION Games

During the summer of 2024 I had an internship at ION Games, a board game studio, working on playtesting and game design. I helped playtest a variety of games, including Dino Dynasty, Atlas, and Pax Viking Second Edition. I worked with the designers to give feedback on rules, balance and rulebooks. Since then I have been in contact with some of the designers and done occasional digital playtests.

Internship

City State (2022)

Turn Based City Builder

https://acasez.itch.io/city-state

City State was made for a my Bachelor's Thesis about structure in Tech Trees. It's a fairly short city building game that was made to see how different tech tree structures of tech trees affect the gameplay. It was however still designed to be fun and playable as game game though and I worked on it afterwards to add some juice.

Solo Project, Made in Unity

To Hel and Back (2021-2022)

Norse mythology inspired hack and slash rougelike.

https://10hpstudios.itch.io/to-hel-and-back

Started as a school project with 10 other students, I worked with game mechanics, balance, UI and level design. 5 of us continued working on it afterwards and it was nominated for the Skövde Academic Game Award (SAGA) in 2021

Made in Unity, Group Project

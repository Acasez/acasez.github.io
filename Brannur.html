Brannur

The brannur are a rare species of robust humanoids, distinguished by their green to gray skin, red eyes, and a single prominent horn on their heads. Known for their formidable combat skills, many brannur have served in battles across the world. Before The Breach, a significant number of them resided in one of the central kingdoms of Avigor. However, compared to gnomes and humans, fewer brannur managed to escape the cataclysmic event. The brannur are known for the Spear’s Legacy, a world-renowned mercenary group with ancient techniques and rites dating back to before The Draconium.

Physical Characteristics

Brannur typically stand between 1.7 and 2.2 meters tall, with well-built, muscular physiques that contribute to their reputation as great fighters. Their skin tones range from green to gray, and their striking red eyes and single horn on their forehead make them visually distinct. They have great vision in low light.

Society and Culture

Community Structure

Brannur society is organized around warrior clans and mercenary bands, with a strong emphasis on honor, discipline, and martial prowess. Leadership is often earned through combat skill and strategic acumen, and elders or veteran warriors typically guide their communities. Their culture values strength, resilience, and loyalty, with a deep respect for tradition and history.


Settlements

Brannur settlements are often fortified and strategically located, reflecting their martial nature. They prefer mountainous and strongholds that can be easily defended, with training grounds and armories as central features. Many brannur live in close-knit communities, where they train the next generation of warriors and preserve their ancient combat techniques.

Economy and Craftsmanship

Brannur are skilled blacksmiths and armorers, crafting high-quality weapons and armor that are sought after by warriors across the world. Their economy thrives on trade related to warfare, including the training of soldiers, the forging of arms, and mercenary contracts. They are also known for their expertise in tactics and strategy, often serving as advisors or commanders in foreign armies.

Religion and Beliefs

Brannur worship a pantheon of warrior deities, with rituals and ceremonies centered around combat, honor, and the ancestors who fell in battle. They believe in proving their worth through deeds and valor, and their spiritual practices often involve trials of strength and endurance.

Daily Life and Practices

Education

Education among the brannur focuses on martial training from a young age. Children are taught combat techniques, strategy, and the history of their people, with an emphasis on discipline and physical conditioning. Storytelling is also a key part of their upbringing, as elders pass down the legends of past warriors and battles.


Art and Music

The brannur have a rich and proud oral tradition, with stories that trace back to the earliest days of Kastorix. This deep-rooted tradition has fostered a talent and passion for drama, theater, and sports within brannur society. Their arts tend to be more action-packed compared to those of other species, reflecting their dynamic and vigorous culture. Epic poems, battle chants, and dramatic reenactments of famous conflicts are common forms of artistic expression.

Interaction with Other Species

Brannur are respected—and sometimes feared—for their combat skills and strategic minds. They often serve as mercenaries, bodyguards, or military advisors, forming alliances with other species based on mutual respect and shared goals. While they are not naturally inclined toward diplomacy, their reputation as formidable warriors ensures that they are often sought after as allies in times of conflict.

Military and Defense

Brannur are known to be great fighters and strategists, and many brannur serve in armies and mercenary companies across the world. Their military tactics are rooted in ancient traditions, blending discipline with adaptability. They excel in both offensive and defensive warfare, often employing phalanx formations and coordinated strikes to overwhelm their enemies. Brannur warriors are trained to fight in various terrains and conditions, making them versatile and reliable in battle.

Magic

While not inherently magical, some brannur practice battle magic, using enchanted weapons and armor to enhance their combat abilities. Their magic is often focused on strength, endurance, and tactical advantage, reflecting their pragmatic and martial culture. Rituals to bless weapons and invoke the favor of their warrior deities are common before major battles.

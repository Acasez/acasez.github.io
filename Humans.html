Humans

Humans used to be most common species in Kastorix before The Breach, who lived in great numbers in countries and cities across Avijor. When those countries fell some made it to Meibor or Santsim, but many died. Humans are diverse in appearance, with a wide range of skin, hair, and eye colors. Known for their physical versatility and ability to adapt to various environments, humans have a remarkable endurance, allowing them to sustain prolonged physical activity longer than many other species.

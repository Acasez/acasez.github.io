Backgrounds

Backgrounds give you a general feat and some other features

Background Name Description Stat Increase
Medic You are practiced in the art of healing and curing. You are trained in medicine and get the Battlefield Medic feat. If you spend your whole turn to do an medicine action you add +3 to that roll. You start with a medicine kit INT or DEX
Noble You come from a wealthy or influential family. You are trained in society and gain the Skilled feat. You start with 15 gold pieces and have a seal from your house. WIL or INT
Soldier You have trained and fought in a army or militia. You are trained in athletics and gain the Combat Training feat. You start with a set of light armor and a martial weapon of your choice. PHY or DEX
Artisan You have trained in a craft or trade. You are trained in crafting and gain the Skilled feat. You start with a set of artisan's tools and 10 gold pieces. INT or DEX
Scholar You have spent years studying a specific field of knowledge. You are trained in arcana, history, or nature and gain the Dedicated Student feat. You start with a book on your field of study and 10 gold pieces. INT or WIL
Explorer You have traveled to many different places and seen many different things. You are trained in survival and gain the Surveyor feat. You start with a set of traveler's clothes, a bedroll, and 10 gold pieces. PHY or WIL
Spy You have worked in the shadows, gathering information and secrets. You are trained in stealth and gain the Sneaky feat. You start with a disguise kit, a forged idenity and 10 gold pieces. DEX or INT
Hunter You've learned how to hunt using traditional ranged weapons or runeguns. You are trained in either bows, crossbows or runeguns. You are also trained in nature or survival. You gain the Take Aim feat. You start with a ranged weapon that you are trained in and 10 gold pieces. DEX or PHY
